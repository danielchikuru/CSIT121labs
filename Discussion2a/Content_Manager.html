Content Manager Job Requirements

Education:

A Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism, English, or a related field is required.

Experience:

Minimum of 3 years of experience in content management, digital marketing, or related fields.

Technical Requirements:

  • Content Management Systems (e.g., WordPress, Drupal)
  • SEO best practices and keyword strategy
  • Proficient in Google Analytics and other web analytics tools
  • Basic HTML and CSS knowledge for content formatting
  • Experience with Email Marketing platforms (e.g., MailChimp, Constant Contact)
  • Social Media Management tools and strategies
  • Understanding of Content Marketing and Online Publishing
  • Image and video editing tools (e.g., Adobe Creative Suite)

Desired Skills:

  • Excellent Writing and Editing Skills
  • Creativity and Innovation in content development
  • Strong Organizational and Time Management abilities
  • Attention to Detail and Quality Assurance
  • Ability to meet deadlines and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Collaboration and Communication skills within a team
