Network Administrator Job Requirements

Education:

Bachelor's or master's degree in computer science, information systems, business administration or related field, or equivalent work experience.

Experience:

Minimum of 2 years of relevant experience in network administration or a related field.

Technical Requirements:

  • Enterprise class routers, switches, firewalls and load balancers (Cisco, Fortinet, Arista).
  • Centralized and field networking experience at an enterprise level (data center, office, clinic/retail/hospitality unit, etc.)
  • In-depth knowledge of TCP/IP (IPv4 & IPv6) as well as DNS and DHCP fundamentals
  • Cloud networking tools and principles (AWS, Azure, GCP, etc.)
  • Deep understanding of network monitoring tools (SolarWinds, Entuity, NetBox, etc.)
  • General understanding in Linux/Windows including scripting experience

Desired Skills:

  • Networking Certifications: CCNA, CCNP. (Preferred).
  • Deep understanding of enterprise network architecture and solutioning.
  • Strong understanding of data center switching/routing as it pertains to network connectivity and physical architecture.
